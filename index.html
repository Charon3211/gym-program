Push Day — Monday

Chest · Shoulders · Triceps

Pro tip: Lateral raises are your #1 width builder — do them every push session, 5 sets. Lead with your elbow, not your hand. Control the descent 2 seconds down.
💪

Pull Day — Tuesday

Back · Biceps · Rear Delts

Pro tip: Deadlift goes first while you're fully fresh. Dead hangs at the end — 2-3 sets to failure. Best thing for your grip and shoulder health.
🦵

Leg Day — Wednesday

Quads · Hamstrings · Glutes · Calves

Pro tip: Big leg compounds drive testosterone — don't skip this thinking it doesn't help your upper body. It does, significantly. Squat deep, control the eccentric.
😴
